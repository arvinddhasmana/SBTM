# 📋 Phase 1 Detailed Requirements (0–12 months)

## 1. **Real‑Time GPS Tracking & Parent Notifications**
- **Hardware:** GPS devices installed on all buses.  
- **Software:** Centralized tracking platform with mobile app for parents and web dashboard for administrators.  
- **Features:**  
  - Live bus location map.  
  - Estimated arrival times.  
  - Push notifications for delays, cancellations, or route deviations.  
- **Compliance:** Ensure data storage complies with Canadian privacy laws (PIPEDA).  

---

## 2. **Driver Mobile Application**
- **Core Functions:**  
  - Turn‑by‑turn navigation optimized for school routes.  
  - Digital student roster with boarding/drop‑off confirmation.  
  - Incident reporting (mechanical issues, student behavior, emergencies).  
- **Security:** Secure login with driver ID and two‑factor authentication.  
- **Integration:** Sync with central system for real‑time updates.  

---

## 3. **Emergency Safety Features**
- **Panic Button:** Installed in buses, linked to OSTA central monitoring and local emergency services.  
- **Geo‑Fencing:** Alerts triggered if bus deviates from assigned route.  
- **Automated Escalation:** Notifications sent to administrators, parents, and law enforcement if required.  

---

## 4. **Video Surveillance Integration (New Requirement)**
- **Hardware:**  
  - High‑definition cameras installed inside buses (front, middle, rear).  
  - Exterior cameras for entry/exit points.  
- **Software:**  
  - Central monitoring system accessible by OSTA administrators.  
  - Secure cloud storage with retention policies (e.g., 30–90 days).  
- **Features:**  
  - Real‑time video feed accessible during emergencies.  
  - Automatic recording triggered by panic button or geo‑fence breach.  
  - AI‑assisted monitoring (detect fights, vandalism, unsafe behavior).  
- **Compliance:**  
  - Adhere to Ontario privacy and surveillance laws.  
  - Clear communication to parents and staff about surveillance use.  
  - Access restricted to authorized personnel only.  
- **Safety Impact:**  
  - Deters bullying, vandalism, and unsafe driver behavior.  
  - Provides evidence for incident investigations.  

---

## 5. **Compliance & Training**
- **Driver Background Checks:** Automated verification of licensing, criminal record, and medical fitness.  
- **Training Modules:** Digital onboarding for safety protocols, emergency handling, and use of new apps.  
- **Legal Alignment:**  
  - Ontario Ministry of Transportation regulations (driver hours, inspections).  
  - School board policies (OCDSB/OCSB).  
  - Accessibility standards for students with disabilities.  

---

# 🎯 Key Deliverables for Phase 1
1. GPS tracking hardware + parent app.  
2. Driver mobile app (navigation + roster + incident reporting).  
3. Emergency safety features (panic button + geo‑fencing).  
4. Video surveillance system (cameras + central monitoring).  
5. Compliance framework (training, background checks, legal alignment).  

---

# ⚡ Expected Outcomes
- **Parents:** Peace of mind with real‑time visibility and safety assurance.  
- **Students:** Safer environment with surveillance and boarding verification.  
- **Drivers:** Easier navigation and immediate support in emergencies.  
- **Administrators:** Centralized control, compliance, and incident evidence.  
- **Community:** Stronger trust in OSTA’s safety and accountability.
