## Your Role in SBTM

As an **Admin** (OSTA Admin or Board Admin), you have platform-wide or board-wide oversight. You use the Admin Dashboard to monitor fleet operations, manage routes and vehicles, review compliance records, and respond to incidents across your scope.

---

## Your Quick-Start Checklist

When you open the Admin Dashboard:

- [ ] Sign in with your admin credentials
- [ ] Review the dashboard tiles for fleet status and alert counts
- [ ] Check the live route activity view for active buses
- [ ] Review any pending emergency alerts
- [ ] Open the Compliance section if reviews are due

## Typical Admin Workflow

1. Sign in and review dashboard status.
2. Open routes, vehicles, or students to verify operational setup.
3. Monitor active alerts and live route activity.
4. Review compliance or audit data when required.
5. Drill into specific school or route issues.
