# SBTM Pricing & Support

## Self-Hosting Costs

SBTM is free to deploy, but you pay for cloud infrastructure. Typical monthly costs:

| District Size | Students | Buses | Azure | GCP | Savings vs SaaS |
|--------------|----------|-------|-------|-----|-----------------|
| Small | 500 | 10 | $250 | $200 | $2,500-5,000/mo |
| Medium | 2,000 | 50 | $400 | $350 | $10,000-20,000/mo |
| Large | 5,000+ | 100+ | $800 | $700 | $25,000-50,000/mo |

**Note**: Costs vary based on usage, data retention, and selected cloud tiers.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I switch plans?
Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect next billing cycle.

### What if I cancel support?
You keep using SBTM free, lose support access. Can reactivate anytime.

### Are there hidden fees?
No. Only cloud infrastructure costs (direct from Azure/GCP) and optional support.

### Do you offer discounts?
Yes! Multi-year commitments, education discounts, and non-profit pricing available.

### Can I pay for support but use community version?
No. Support plans include the software. But software is free without support.

### What about updates?
Security updates are free for all. Feature updates included with support plans.
