## Tips for Beta Recruitment

1. **Target Size:** 3-5 districts (manageable feedback)

2. **Ideal Beta Testers:**
   - Small-medium districts (500-2,000 students)
   - Tech-savvy IT staff
   - Willing to try new things
   - Good communicators
   - Frustrated with current solution

3. **Where to Find Them:**
   - School administrator LinkedIn groups
   - Education technology conferences
   - Local school district contacts
   - AASA, NSTA, ASBO memberships
   - Cold outreach to transportation coordinators

4. **Red Flags:**
   - Very large districts (too complex for beta)
   - No technical staff
   - Risk-averse culture
   - No decision-making authority

5. **Follow-Up Cadence:**
   - Initial email
   - Follow-up #1: 3 days
   - Follow-up #2: 7 days after #1
   - Then stop (don't spam)

6. **Track Metrics:**
   - Open rate
   - Response rate
   - Conversion rate
   - Iterate on messaging
